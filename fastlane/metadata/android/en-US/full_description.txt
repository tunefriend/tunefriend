TuneFriend lets you stream music from a friend's self-hosted music server on your phone.

Works with Navidrome, Airsonic, Gonic, Ampache, and other Subsonic-compatible servers.

Features:
• Browse artists, albums, and songs
• Search your friend's library
• Shuffle all or play random tracks
• Background playback with lock-screen controls
• Connect to any Subsonic-compatible server

Your friend needs to give you their server URL, username, and password. TuneFriend connects directly — no account with us required.
