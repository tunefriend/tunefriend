## TuneFriend v2.21

Fix favorite albums not opening and more songs failing to play.

### Changes

- **Favorite albums** — tapping an album now opens it (was loading behind the favorites screen)
- **Back from favorite album** — returns to Favorites, not Home
- **Transcode fallback** — if the original file won't play on your phone (FLAC, etc.), automatically retries as MP3
