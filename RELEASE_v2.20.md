## TuneFriend v2.20

Fix favorites and shuffle playback stopping or requiring many skips.

### Changes

- **Fresh stream URLs** on every play, skip, and recovery (Subsonic auth tokens expire)
- **Auto-skip** tracks that fail twice instead of getting stuck
- **Native auto-skip** bad streams in the background queue
- **Fewer race conditions** when skipping quickly or resuming the app
