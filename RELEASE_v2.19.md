## TuneFriend v2.19

Fix overnight playback stopping after long background sessions.

### Changes

- **Audio focus fix** — stop registering duplicate focus listeners on each track change (could pause playback hours later)
- **Auto-resume** — native player resumes when audio focus returns after alarms/notifications
- **Wake recovery** — when you open the app, playback resumes if it was paused by the system overnight
