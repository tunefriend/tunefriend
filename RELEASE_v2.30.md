## TuneFriend v2.30

### Playlists
- **Playlists** button in the top bar (next to Favorites & Settings)
- Create named playlists, rename, delete
- **Add artist** — search your synced library and add that artist’s songs in one tap
- Play All / Shuffle per playlist; remove individual tracks
- Favorites screen also links to Playlists so you have Songs + Playlists in one place

Playlists are saved on the device (separate from heart Favorites).
